Product Description

Discover the timeless wisdom of Epictetus, a remarkable figure born into slavery around 55 CE in the eastern outreaches of the Roman Empire. Following his emancipation, he went on to establish an influential school of Stoic philosophy, leaving a lasting legacy. Epictetus's teachings revolve around the profound idea that while we cannot control life's circumstances, we have the power to control our responses to them.

In his masterpiece, "The Art of Living," he imparts ninety-three witty, wise, and razor-sharp instructions that provide readers with the tools to navigate the challenges of everyday life with skill and wisdom. Through these teachings, individuals not only learn to overcome life's hurdles but also to confront inevitable losses and disappointments with grace and resilience.
